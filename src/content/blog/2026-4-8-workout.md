Here are some of my preferred track workouts, mostly for half-marathon / marathon distances.

1. 3 speed / 5 speed. 

3 speed. 1 minute at marathon pace, 1 minute at 10k pace, 1 minute at 5k pace. 

5 speed: 1 minute at marathon pace, 1 minute at half marathon pace, 1 minute at 10k pace, 1 minute at 5k pace, 1 minute at 2 mile pace. 

Repeat either for 20-35 minutes.

If you've never raced these distances, the marathon pace is about as fast as you can go while still feeling like you can sustain it for a long time. Then for everything after that, take 15-30 seconds off that time depending on how you feel. Thanks Carl from Strawberry Canyon for this one!

2. 800 on/off

I think it's good here to keep the "off" pretty fast. For example i might do 2:40 / 3:15 or something. 6-8 reps, more during peak training time.

It's kind of interesting to compare these to Yasso's, I generally like doing harder reps / more rest, but I definitely notice better results with more consistent effort workouts.

3. Tempo

I do like 4-6 miles usually. Less if I'm out of shape. Around half marathon pace.

4. Progression

This is just totally vibes based. Just end it faster than your normal tempo.
